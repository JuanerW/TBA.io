Step 2: Setting Up

Follow these simple steps to set up and start optimizing your productivity:

Sign Up / Log In

Sign Up:

  • Visit our website or open the Task Booster Assistance app.
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button.
  • Fill in the required information such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
Log In:
  • If you already have an account, simply click on the "Log In" button.
  • Enter your email address and password to access your dashboard.

Set Up Your Profile

Personal Information:

  • Once logged in, navigate to your profile settings.
  • Fill in your personal details like your name, job title, and a profile picture.
Preferences:
  • Set your task management preferences, including default urgency and importance levels, time zone, and notification settings.

Add Your First Task

Navigate to 'Add Task':

  • From your dashboard, click on the "Add Task" button.
Enter Task Details:
  • Task Name: Provide a clear and descriptive name for the task.
  • Time Required (minutes): Estimate how long it will take to complete the task.
  • Urgency: Select the urgency level of the task (e.g., Low, Medium, High).
  • Importance: Specify the importance level (e.g., Low, Medium, High).
  • Category: Choose the relevant category for the task (e.g., Work, Personal, Study).
Save Task:
  • Click on the "Save" button to add the task to your task list.

Step 3: Implementation

Welcome to Task Booster Assistance, a cutting-edge productivity tool designed to revolutionize the way you manage your tasks. When adding a task, users will be prompted to set the following details:

  • Task Name: Clearly identify the task.
  • Time Required (minutes): Estimate the duration needed to complete the task.
  • Urgency: Indicate the urgency level of the task.
  • Importance: Specify the importance of the task.
  • Category: Classify the task into a relevant category.
